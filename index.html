Can i haz codes?!

example
is a popular meme between programmers when someone asks a question that basically has the statement „This is what it has to look like, now write the code for me.“

Nope, we won’t do that. But we will help to get it right!

  • Try it on your own first

    Include your own try to the question, then proceed with the following steps. If you din't try anything yet, you won't get help.

  • Explain your problem

    Describe what exactly is not working. Include error messages and explain the behaviour of your program.

  • Tell us your goal

    Summarize what you want to achieve and what your program should do once it's working.

  • Just relevant information

    Take your time to decide what information is relevant to your question and what is not. Post only relevant code.

  • No images!

    Never ever post code / error messages / etc. as images. Post them as text instead.

  • Correct tagging

    Always tag your question correctly. Don't mix something up just because it sounds similar.
